Biography

Indra Nooyi is an Indian-American business executive who served as the CEO and Chairperson of PepsiCo, one of the world's largest food and beverage companies. Under her leadership, PepsiCo's revenue grew by more than 80%, and she was instrumental in reshaping the company with a focus on healthier products. Known for her strategic vision and leadership, she has been consistently ranked among the world's most powerful women.

Indra Nooyi’s journey from Chennai to leading a global corporation is a testament to her tenacity, brilliance, and determination. Her work continues to inspire many aspiring entrepreneurs and leaders around the world, proving that with hard work and vision, one can reach the pinnacle of success.
